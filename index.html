Method

How the site selection engine works

01

Business-aware candidate generation

The app builds nearby candidate zones around your target city, then adjusts the search logic based on your business type and supplier dependence.

02

Maps and cluster intelligence

Google Maps hooks can geocode the area and estimate competitors, supplier nodes, transit access, parking, and complementary businesses around every site.

03

Census-backed demographic layer

Population reach and spending power are mapped into each option. When live mode is disabled or unsupported, the module falls back to demo values so the website still works.

04

MCDA scoring and forecast

Every site is normalized across benefit and cost criteria, scored with weighted MCDA, and paired with a land-price projection so you can balance present fit with future upside.
